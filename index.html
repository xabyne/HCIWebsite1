Introduction

I am Xabyne and I am studing Communication and Multimedia Design at The Hague University of Applied Sciences. Currently I am in my second year.
I love to sport, chill with friends, watch series and listen to music.
Furthermore I like to create designs. The challenge to create something to make life of people easier is something that really interests me.

Expectations

My expectation is that I am going to learn to create interactive concepts. I suppose that this course has a strong focus on technology. I think I am going to learn a lot about different technologies, such as wearables, playful interfaces, augmented and virtual reality and artificial creatures. In de Lab weeks I think I am going to learn how to create something physical.

What I want to learn

I want to learn about the possibilies of technology and how to create a detailed concept. Also I want to know what the possibilities of wearables, playful interfaces, augmented and virtual reality and artificial creatures. Furthermore I want to learn how to use the HCI-kit. I can't wait to learn how to create a concept that is based on so much technology.
